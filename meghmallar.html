Synopsis

Meghmallar a story of fear, courage, despair, and hope in the time of war. It was the monsoon of 1971. The Liberation War for Bangladesh had started to gain momentum. In this testing time an ordinary family goes through a life altering experience in a span of just three days. Meghmallar narrates this tragic tale.

The main protagonist of this tale is Nurul Huda, a chemistry teacher of a Government college in a provincial town. His middle class existence revolves around his wife Asma and 5 years old daughter Sudha. They have another person living with them, Mintu, Asmaâ€™s brother.

Suddenly one morning they find that Mintu has left, without informing anyone, to join the freedom fighters. This unexpected turn of event puts Nurul Huda in a crisis. But he tries to act normal by attending the college regularly. He even panders to his pro-Pakistani colleagues. Plagued by fear, dilemma and confusion, that defines his middle class identity; he finds it difficult to cope with the situation.

In a night of heavy rain freedom fighters attack the Pakistani Army Camp located in the college campus. In retaliation, the Army comes to take Nurul Huda into their custody. While they are taking him away, Asma hands Huda a raincoat left by Mintu, to protect him from the rain.

Initially Huda is terrified while facing the army. He tries to establish that he is no more than a teacher and has no association with the freedom fighters. But gradually, intensity of the torture increases. Nurul Huda starts bleeding from the nose. Looking at the blood reminds Huda of Mintu and suddenly he goes through a transformation. He imagines himself as a freedom fighter, who would not hesitate sacrificing his own life for the country. He shouts that he knows everything but will reveal nothing. One bullet from the Major silences him.

After a long struggle, the country is liberated. But not without sacrifices of many. Asma and Sudha are two such individuals. Nurul Huda is dead, but it is the living, who continues to suffer with wounded hearts.
